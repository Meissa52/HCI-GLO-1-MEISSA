Alfred State College: Future Activities Survey

Hello and welcome. Alfred State is currently trying to expand its social activities on-campus for all current and incoming students. We felt that Alfred State has a lot to offer and we need to know what students want so we can deliver. Below is a optional survey that will give us an idea of what students want, like, and dislike.

Are you currently enrolled at Alfred State?
What building do you currently live in?
Are you a member of any clubs or organizations on-campus?
How many on-campus events did you attend last week?
How do you find out about the events that you do go to?
Would you go to more events if food/drinks were provided?
What is your favorite type of event?
Where do you prefer events to be held?
Would you be more interested in events if they were free?
Please tell us how you think we could improve our events.
